## Status Vocabulary

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `submitted` | Application sent — no response yet |
| `active` | Interviewing — a next step is scheduled or in progress |
| `pending` | A round / loop is complete — awaiting result or decision |
| `rejected` | No longer in process (rejected) |
| `withdrawn` | Withdrew from the process |
| `ghosted` | Recruiter / company went silent after engagement |
