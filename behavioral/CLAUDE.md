# behavioral/

## Purpose

STAR-method stories and leadership principle responses for staff-level behavioral rounds. At staff level, interviewers assess scope of influence, cross-functional leadership, and technical judgment under ambiguity — not just individual execution.

## Planned Contents

- `stories.md` — Bank of STAR stories mapped to common behavioral themes
- Per-company leadership principles (e.g., Amazon LPs, Google attributes)

## Conventions

- Each story: **Situation → Task → Action → Result** with quantified impact where possible.
- Tag each story with the themes it covers (e.g., `[conflict]`, `[ambiguity]`, `[influence]`, `[failure]`).
- Keep results specific: team size, timeline, business impact, metric improvement.

## Key Themes to Cover

- Driving alignment across teams without authority
- Handling technical disagreement with senior stakeholders
- Making a call under ambiguity or incomplete data
- Delivering a project that had significant obstacles
- Mentoring, growing, or unblocking others
- Pushing back on a bad idea (and being right, or being wrong)
- Navigating competing priorities or org changes
