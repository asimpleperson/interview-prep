# Riot Games — Company Research

## What They Do
Riot Games makes League of Legends, Valorant, and other titles. Engineering focuses on game backend infrastructure, real-time systems, and player-facing platform services at global scale.

## Principal Software Engineer Role
Principal-level at Riot is a senior staff / principal IC track. Responsibilities span technical strategy, cross-team architecture, and high-impact execution.

## What They Value
- Player-first thinking — engineering decisions framed around player experience
- Deep technical ownership
- Collaborative culture; "Rioters" emphasis on humility and team trust
- Global scale: 150M+ registered players, datacenter presence worldwide

## Domain Topics to Know
- Low-latency game server infrastructure (matchmaking, game state sync)
- Global CDN and content delivery for game clients and patches
- Anti-cheat and fraud detection systems
- Real-time communication (voice, chat) at scale
- Player data platforms (telemetry, analytics, personalization)
