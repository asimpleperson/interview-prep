## What to Add Per Company

When preparing for a specific company, populate its folder with:
- `overview.md` — Company mission, products, engineering culture, recent news
- `process.md` — Interview format, number of rounds, known focus areas
- `questions.md` — Known or reported interview questions
- `values.md` — Leadership principles or engineering values (especially for Amazon)
- Any company-specific system design or domain notes

## Conventions

- Each company folder has its own `CLAUDE.md` describing its current prep status.
- Update the Stage column above when a company moves from Placeholder → Active → Done.
