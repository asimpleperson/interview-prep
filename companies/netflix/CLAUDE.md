# Netflix — Company Research

## What They Do
Global streaming and entertainment platform. Netflix engineering is known for its microservices culture, chaos engineering (Chaos Monkey), and high-autonomy teams.

## Software Engineer 5 — Ads Pricing & Packaging
SE5 is a senior-to-staff equivalent at Netflix. The Ads Pricing & Packaging team owns how ad inventory is priced, packaged, and sold — sitting at the intersection of ads infrastructure, revenue, and business logic.
