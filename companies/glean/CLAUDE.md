# Glean — Company Research

## What They Do
Enterprise AI search and knowledge platform. Indexes company data across 100+ SaaS tools (Slack, Drive, Jira, Confluence, Salesforce, etc.) and surfaces answers using LLMs grounded in internal knowledge.

## Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) Role
FDEs sit at the intersection of engineering and customer success. Responsibilities typically include:
- Onboarding enterprise customers and driving technical integration
- Customizing Glean to customer workflows (connectors, permissions, prompts)
- Feeding product insights back to the core engineering team
- Writing lightweight code / scripts to solve customer-specific problems
- Acting as a trusted technical advisor to customer engineering teams
