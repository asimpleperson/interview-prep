# TikTok — Company Research

## What They Do
TikTok is ByteDance's short-video platform. The Monetization Technology org builds the ads infrastructure powering TikTok's revenue: ad serving, auction, targeting, measurement, and creator monetization.

## Tech Lead / Senior SWE — Monetization Technology
A tech lead role here spans both IC and leadership responsibilities:
- Owning technical direction for a monetization sub-system (auction, targeting, billing, reporting)
- Writing and reviewing high-impact code in a high-scale environment
- Coordinating across ads, data, and product teams
- Mentoring engineers and driving design reviews

## What They Value
- Systems thinking at ads scale (millions of QPS, sub-100ms SLA)
- Strong algorithm foundation (ByteDance is known for hard LeetCode)
- Technical leadership: RFC-level design, mentorship, cross-team influence
- Metrics and experimentation mindset (A/B testing, revenue impact)

## Domain Topics to Know
- Ad auction mechanics (second-price, VCG, pacing)
- Real-time bidding (RTB) and DSP/SSP architecture
- Click/impression tracking pipelines
- Ad targeting: user segmentation, lookalike audiences, contextual signals
- Measurement and attribution (multi-touch, view-through)
- Fraud detection in ad networks
