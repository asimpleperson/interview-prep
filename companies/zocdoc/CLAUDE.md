# Zocdoc — Company Research

## What They Do
Zocdoc is a healthcare marketplace connecting patients to doctors for appointment booking. Engineering focuses on marketplace reliability, patient/provider data, and healthcare integrations (EHR, insurance eligibility).

- Reliability and trust — healthcare data is sensitive and mission-critical

- Healthcare marketplace: supply (providers) / demand (patients) matching
- EHR integrations and HL7/FHIR standards
- Insurance eligibility and benefits verification
- Appointment scheduling and calendar systems
- HIPAA compliance and PHI data handling
